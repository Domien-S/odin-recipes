pizzadough recipe

Description

Today we will make quick and easy pizzadough. This pizzadough is a great start to making your own delicious pizza from scratch!.

Ingredient list

  • 500 grams of flower
  • 0,5 spoons of salt
  • 1 7 grams packet of dry yeast
  • 0,5 teaspoons of sugar
  • 325 ml of lukewarm water

Cooking steps

  1. Disolve the sugar in the water and add the yeast. When everything is well mixed, let it rest for 15 minutes.
  2. Mix the flower with the salt. Add the yeast mixture and knead till you have one homogenous mass. Knead for at least 10 minutes.
  3. Make two balls from the dough for two large pizzas. You can also make three or four smaller pizzas.
  4. Let the doughballs rest for 15 minutes.
  5. Flatten the doughballs and put them on a pizza form or a cast iron pann.
  6. You are now ready to finish your pizza!
