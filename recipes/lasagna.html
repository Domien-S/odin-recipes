Lasagna recipe

Description

Today we will make a healthy vegan lasagne. This lasagna is super delicious and provides plenty of fiber and protein.

Ingredient list

  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 200 grams cooked lentils
  • 400 grams of diced pumpkin
  • 400 grams of diced tomatoes
  • 2 spoons of Italian herbs
  • 125 gram of wholegrain lasagna leafs
  • 75 grams of vegan grated cheese
  • one spoon of olive oil
  • 125 ml water
  • salt and pepper to taste
