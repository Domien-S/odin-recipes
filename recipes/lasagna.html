Lasagna recipe

Description

Today we will make a healthy vegan lasagne. This lasagna is super delicious and provides plenty of fiber and protein.

Ingredient list

  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 200 grams cooked lentils
  • 400 grams of diced pumpkin
  • 400 grams of diced tomatoes
  • 2 spoons of Italian herbs
  • 125 gram of wholegrain lasagna leafs
  • 75 grams of vegan grated cheese
  • one spoon of olive oil
  • 125 ml water
  • salt and pepper to taste

Cooking steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C
  2. Peel and dice the onion and the garlic.
  3. Heat up the olive oil in a big stovetop and glaze the onion and garlic for two minutes
  4. Add the diced pumpkin, the lentils, the tomatoes and the water. Leave on medium heat for 8 minutes.
  5. Remember to taste the recipe and season as you go.
  6. Take some of the sauce and put it into an oven casserole. Then add a layer of the lasagna leaves. Repeat untill the casserole is almost full or untill you are out of sauce.
  7. To finish put the grated cheese on top.
  8. Bake in the oven for 25 minutes.
